LightWAVE and PhysioNetWorks

George Moody
Last revised: 22 February 2013 (0.48)

This page describes features that are not yet implemented on PhysioNetWorks!

PhysioNetWorks workspaces are available to members of the PhysioNet community for works in progress that will be made publicly available via PhysioNet when complete. Unlike other areas of PhysioNet, these workspaces are password-protected.

LightWAVE can provide access to PhysioBank-compatible data within PhysioNetWorks projects to which you belong.

Follow these steps to set up access:

  1. If you are the owner of a private or shared PhysioNetWorks project that contains compatible data, go to your project's Manage page and click on . Follow the instructions to complete the setup. Repeat this step for any other private or shared projects with compatible data.
  2. From your PhysioNetWorks home page, click on .
  3. In the future, when you upload additional compatible data into one of your projects, go to its Manage page and click on .

Follow these steps to view PhysioNetWorks data:

  1. There are two ways to access compatible data in PhysioNetWorks via LightWAVE:
    • Launch the preconfigured LightWAVE for PhysioNetWorks client at https://physionet.org/users/shared/lightwave/.
    • Launch the standard LightWAVE client at http://physionet.org/lightwave/ or any desired custom version of it, and enter the URL of the LightWAVE for PhysioNetWorks server (https://physionet.org/pnw/a/lightwave/) as the server on the client's Settings tab.
  2. Log in to PhysioNetWorks if you haven't done so already.
